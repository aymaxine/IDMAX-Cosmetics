About IDMAX Cosmetics

Bringing you quality products since 2016

Our Story

IDMAX House, the umbrella company, began with a heartfelt vision: to create a shopping destination where quality meets purpose, and self-care becomes a celebration of identity.

Founded on September 15, 2016, by a devoted mother inspired by her five children — four radiant daughters and one spirited son — IDMAX Cosmetics was born out of love and a deep desire to nurture confidence. Witnessing the beauty and uniqueness of her children's skin, she wanted to instill in them a sense of pride, care, and self-worth through products that encourage people to embrace and invest in their natural beauty.

What started as a passion project in a family home has grown into a trusted online brand, cherished by customers around the world. Every product we offer is curated with intention — to reflect our commitment to quality, inclusivity, and confidence-building care. We've come a long way, but our mission remains the same: to celebrate individuality and empower every customer to feel seen, valued, and beautiful in their own skin.

With an ever-expanding catalog and thousands of happy customers, IDMAX Cosmetics continues to thrive on its core values: integrity, innovation, and heartfelt service. This is more than shopping — it's a journey of self-love, one order at a time.

Our Mission & Values

We carefully curate our products to ensure they meet the highest standards of quality and durability. Each item in our catalog undergoes thorough testing and verification before reaching our customers.
